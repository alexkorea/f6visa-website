## F-6 Renewal Overview

The F-6 (Marriage Immigration) visa must be renewed before expiry. Renewal requires proof that the marriage is genuine and ongoing.

## Required Documents

| # | Document | Note |
|---|----------|------|
| 1 | Extension application form | Immigration Office form |
| 2 | Passport and ARC | |
| 3 | Detailed marriage certificate | Issued within 3 months |
| 4 | Korean spouse's resident registration | Issued within 3 months |
| 5 | Family relation certificate | Issued within 3 months |
| 6 | Co-habitation proof | Joint registration or lease |
| 7 | Couples' recent photos | Within last 1 year |

## Proving Marriage Authenticity

If authenticity is questioned, officers may request additional documents or conduct an interview. Supplementary evidence: messaging history, bank transfers between spouses, family gathering photos.

## Children as Supporting Evidence

Having a Korean-national child strengthens your renewal case. Submit the child's basic certificate and family relation certificate.
