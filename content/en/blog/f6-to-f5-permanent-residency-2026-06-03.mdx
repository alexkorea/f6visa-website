## Converting F-6 to F-5

F-6 (Marriage Immigrant) visa holders who maintain their marriage and meet specific requirements can convert to F-5 (Permanent Residency).

## Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Marriage | Must maintain marriage with Korean national |
| Stay Period | At least 2 years of residence on F-6 status |
| Income | Household income above per capita GNI |
| Compliance | No immigration law violations |

## Special Exception (Child Rearing)

If raising a minor child born from marriage with a Korean national in Korea, income requirement is waived.

## Required Documents

1. Application for change of status
2. Passport and ARC
3. Marriage certificate (Korean spouse)
4. Income proof (withholding tax receipt, etc.)
5. Family relations certificate
6. 2 photos

## Processing Time

Typically 4–8 weeks. Complex cases may take longer.
