## F-6 Visa and Employment

The F-6 (Marriage Immigrant) visa grants foreign spouses of Korean nationals the right to **work freely without a separate work permit**. This is a significant advantage over work-specific visas.

## Eligible Occupations

F-6 visa holders can work in virtually any profession, from manual labor to specialized fields. Exceptions include positions requiring Korean citizenship (government officials, national security roles).

## Employment Process

1. Sign an employment contract with a registered company
2. Enroll in 4 social insurances (National Pension, Health, Employment, Industrial Accident)
3. File annual income tax return

## Self-Employment

F-6 holders can register as sole proprietors and run their own businesses. Tax filing obligations apply after business registration.
