## F-6 Visa Renewal Basics

It is recommended to apply for renewal **within 4 months before expiry**. Under Article 25 of the Immigration Act, renewal must be filed before the expiration date.

## Required Documents

1. Application for extension of stay
2. Original passport and Alien Registration Card
3. Marriage certificate (Korean spouse's record)
4. Korean spouse's resident registration certificate
5. Proof of cohabitation (utility bills, lease agreement, etc.)
6. Family relations certificate

## Proving a Genuine Marriage

Immigration authorities verify not just legal marriage but **genuine cohabitation**:
- Living together at the same address
- Presence of children
- Communication with spouse (basic interview in Korean or common language)

## Processing Time and Duration

- Processing: **1–2 weeks** (varies by office)
- Duration granted: 1 or 2 years depending on assessment
