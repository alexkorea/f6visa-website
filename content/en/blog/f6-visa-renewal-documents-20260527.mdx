# F-6 Marriage Visa Renewal Documents 2026 — Complete Checklist

F-6 marriage immigration visa holders must apply for renewal before their stay period expires.
The renewal review checks whether the marriage is still intact, the sponsor's current income, and the couple's place of residence.
This guide lists all required documents for F-6 visa renewal in 2026.

## When to Apply for Renewal

- **Earliest:** Up to 4 months (120 days) before expiry
- **Recommended:** 2–3 months before expiry
- **After expiry:** Strictly not accepted — must apply before the expiry date

Processing typically takes 2–4 weeks, longer during peak seasons.

## Required Documents

**Common Documents (Always Required)**

- Application for extension of stay (per Immigration Act Enforcement Regulations form)
- Passport (original)
- Alien registration card (original)
- Photo (3.5×4.5 cm)
- Application fee

**Marriage Continuity Proof**

- Resident registration certificate of Korean spouse (within 3 months)
- Family relations certificate (within 3 months)
- Proof of cohabitation: utility bills (same address), rental contract, etc.
- Proof of couple interaction: family photos, shared social media (if requested)

**Sponsor Income Documents**

- Withholding tax certificate (previous year)
- Health insurance premium payment confirmation
- For self-employed: income tax payment certificate or VAT base proof

If income is insufficient, a guarantor (family member) can supplement with their income documentation.

## Additional Documents for Special Situations

**If you have children**
- Child's birth certificate or family relations certificate

**If currently separated or with separation history**
- Documentation explaining reason for separation
- Communication records during separation period

**If Korean spouse has passed away or divorced (F-6-2 application)**
- Death certificate or divorce judgment
- Documents proving you are raising children in Korea (if applicable)

## Common Rejection Reasons

- Marriage relationship broken or suspected sham marriage
- Sponsor income below threshold
- Tax arrears
- Legal violations during stay period

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1. Who issues the family relations certificate?**

It must be issued in the name of the Korean spouse. Obtainable at a local community center (주민센터) or via Government24 online.

**Q2. Can I travel abroad during the renewal process?**

Departing Korea after filing for renewal may result in the application being withdrawn. It's safer to wait for the renewal to be completed before traveling.

**Q3. Do I need new home country documents for renewal?**

Home country documents are typically not required again for renewal. However, additional documents may be requested in specific circumstances.
