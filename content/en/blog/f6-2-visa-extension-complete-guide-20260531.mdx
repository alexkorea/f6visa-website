# F-6-2 Visa Extension: Complete Step-by-Step Guide

## When to Apply

The F-6-2 visa is typically valid for 1–2 years. You must apply for an extension within 4 months before expiration up to the expiration date. Missing this window results in illegal overstay status.

## Required Documents

Extension application form, passport, alien registration card, family relationship certificate, marriage certificate, Korean spouse's family register, and proof of income and assets.

## Proving Cohabitation

The key to F-6-2 extension is proving your marriage is genuine and ongoing. Evidence includes a combined household registration, shared financial records, and photographs.

## If Separated or Divorcing

Extensions may still be granted in cases of separation or ongoing divorce proceedings if certain conditions are met. Expert guidance is strongly recommended in these situations.
