# F-6-1 Marriage Visa Embassy Interview: Complete Preparation Guide

## Why the Interview Matters

When applying for an F-6-1 (Spouse of Korean National) visa, you must attend an interview at a Korean embassy or consulate. The interviewer will assess the genuineness of your marriage and the eligibility of the Korean sponsor.

## Common Interview Questions

Expect questions about how you met your spouse, the timeline of your relationship, and your plans for living in Korea. Specific, consistent, and honest answers that demonstrate your genuine relationship are essential.

## Document Checklist for Interview Day

Bring your passport, visa application form, translated and notarized marriage certificate and family register, and the Korean sponsor's guarantee letter.

## Key Precautions

Both spouses' answers must be consistent. False statements can result in visa denial and future entry bans.
