# F-6 Visa Extension for Pregnancy and Childbirth 2026

Pregnancy and recent childbirth are positive factors in F-6 visa extension reviews — and in some cases allow extension even after the visa has expired.

## Extending During Pregnancy

Submit at least 4 months before expiry if possible.

Documents needed:
- Integrated application form
- Passport and alien registration card
- Pregnancy confirmation letter (from OB/GYN clinic)
- Proof of cohabitation with Korean spouse

## Extending After Childbirth

Even if the visa has expired, immigration accepts childbirth as grounds for an exception extension.

Additional documents:
- Birth certificate or hospital birth confirmation
- Child's passport (or birth certificate if passport not yet issued)

## Key Point

Apply as early as possible. If already in overstay status due to childbirth, report to immigration with supporting documents — penalties are reduced for documented birth situations.

Extension granted: **1 year** stay period.
