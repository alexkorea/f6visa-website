# F-6 Marriage Visa Renewal in Korea 2026

The F-6 marriage immigration status is renewable as long as a genuine marital relationship with a Korean spouse is maintained.

## When to Apply

- Applications open **4 months before** the expiry date
- Staying past the expiry date constitutes illegal overstay

## Required Documents

1. Integrated application form
2. Passport and alien registration card
3. Korean spouse's resident registration (Jumindeungnok)
4. Family relations certificate (showing marriage)
5. Spouse's income proof (health insurance contribution certificate or withholding tax certificate)
6. Cohabitation evidence — joint lease, photos, communication records, etc.

## Common Issue

If living separately, proving genuine marriage is difficult. Submit a written explanation of the reason (spouse's workplace, children's schooling, etc.) as supporting documentation.
