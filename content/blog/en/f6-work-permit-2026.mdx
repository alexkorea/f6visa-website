# Can F-6 Marriage Visa Holders Work in Korea? 2026 Guide

Holders of the F-6 (marriage immigration) status are permitted to engage in almost all types of employment under the Enforcement Decree of the Immigration Act — no separate work permit required.

## Permitted Work

- Corporate employment, public service, self-employment, freelancing — all allowed
- No separate work permit needed
- Business registration is also permitted

## Restrictions

- Adult entertainment industry employment is restricted
- Gambling-related industries are restricted

## Health Insurance & Social Security

Upon employment, you enroll in workplace health insurance. This also serves as useful income proof when renewing the F-6 visa.

## Starting a Business

Register with the local tax office and start operating immediately. No change of visa status is required.
