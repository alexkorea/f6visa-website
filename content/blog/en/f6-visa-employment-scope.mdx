# F-6 Marriage Visa Employment Rights Korea 2026

The F-6 (Marriage Immigration) visa grants open work authorization in Korea. No separate work permit is required.

## What You Can Do on F-6

| Activity | Allowed |
|----------|---------|
| Employment | Yes, unrestricted |
| Business registration | Yes |
| Freelance work | Yes |
| Corporate representative | Yes |

## Business Registration Process

1. Apply via Hometax or visit a tax office
2. Present F-6 alien registration card
3. Submit lease agreement for business premises
4. Receive business registration certificate (3–5 days)

## FAQ

**Q. Do I need to notify immigration when I start working?**
A. No separate notification is required for F-6 holders.

**Q. Are there any industries I cannot work in on F-6?**
A. There are no industry restrictions for F-6 holders, unlike some other visa categories.
